## Problem

The team had a hypothesis: a segment of our customer base would pay a premium for features that don't exist yet. Leadership wanted to build. Engineering wanted to know it was worth it.

## Approach

Rather than commissioning months of research or building and hoping, we ran a lean validation experiment: a simple waitlist page with a clear value proposition, behind a real sign-up gate.

No product. No promise of timeline. Just: "We're exploring this. Want to be first?"

## Process

- Built the landing page in two weeks with minimal engineering involvement
- Drove traffic via in-app messaging and email to a targeted segment
- Set a threshold: if we hit 5,000 sign-ups in 30 days, we'd greenlight the build
